About Us

Sri Sai Group is among India's top private sector business houses serving a very satisfied customers across, realty, hospitality & entertainment, and Information technology, Media & Digital Marketing sectors.

Founded by the Shri Naresh Dhoundiyal, the Sri Sai Group positively influences the lives of the aspiring Indians across Delhi, Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, Noida, Greater Noida, Gaziabad & Ahemdabad.

The Sri Sai Group strongly believes that it has a pivotal role to play in shaping the destiny of our great nation. Through its various consumer-facing businesses, the Group provides a robust platform to every Indian to realize his/ her potential through its state-of-the-art products and services various sectors like Realty, Information Technology, Hospitality & Entertainment. The Group enjoys the unparalleled trust, faith and confidence of its customers & business associates/partners and is one of the Group in the country with a young, highly-trained and motivated workforce.

Group Housing Development

Our construction companies, Mangalaya Buildtech pvt. ltd & Sri Sai Shiv Developers LLP aim to breathes life into your dreams, makes houses into homes, Homes into heaven. We do not merely construct buildings, we create homes for living. As rightly set out in our vision and mission statements our companies envisages entering into the elite group of top corporate in the field of real estate developments to fullfill the aims and objectives. A strong foundation and an equally strong commitment to the highest standard of quality and ethics has helped us establish ourselves as the premier constriction company of Delhi NCR. All our constriction is testimony for quality, timely delivery and trustworthy relationships with our customers, who get both living and breathing. The group is known for its outstanding quality, timely delivery and excellent customer services. The company is envisioned to re-define luxury and fine living. Projects located in Delhi NCR and Uttar Pradesh is inspired by Spanish style architecture with state-of-art amenities.

Brokerage

LAB Realty Private Limited is one of India's biggest platforms for Mortgages and Real estate and is one of India's fastest growing property search platforms. At LAB Realty Private Limited, we believe that commitment, honesty, trust, and dedication are the four pillars that can describe our values and ethics for working in the real estate industry. The company is incorporated with the sole purpose of providing cost-effective and best-in-class solutions in real estate. LAB Realty Private Limited platform offers the best integrated client experience. It is also built to provide assistance for the complete real-estate journey from discovering home loans, rental, search, transactions, post-sale services, and property management

Entertainment & Hospitality

Each of our projects is a unique testament to the World of Entertainment; be it - Snow World, The Game, XOXO – The Casual Lounge, Pink Wasabi and many more. The projects under the group umbrella are prudently outlined to provide a perfect blend of adrenaline rush and elation. Acts as a conduit for innovation and creativity in those essential sectors of businesses in India, which inherit a personal need of an Individual.
